Hiking

is an enjoyable physical activity practiced by many people around the world. It combines exercise with enjoying the beauty of nature, and it helps people escape daily life pressures while spending calm and beneficial time.

most of doctors recommended walking or hiking because of full of benefits , there are some :

  • Improves physical fitness
  • Reduces stress and improves mood
  • Helps with weight control
  • Increases energy
  • Improves sleep
  • Builds confidence

To avoid boredom, try going out with a small group or with close friends, as it is also better for general safety

It is also advisable to stay well hydrated and eat protein-rich foods, as they help you walk for longer periods

In conclusion, walking and going out are good for your health and make you feel happy. When you go out with friends and make sure to drink water and eat good food, you can enjoy your time and stay active and safe.
